# Privacy Policy for Aspend

**Effective Date:** February 13, 2026

Aspend ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information.

## 1. Data Privacy & Local Storage
Aspend is designed with a "Privacy First" philosophy. 
- **Offline by Design**: All financial data, including transactions, categories, and account information, is stored **locally on your device**.
- **No Cloud Sync**: We do not use any cloud servers to store or sync your financial data. 
- **User Control**: You have full control over your data. You can delete all data at any time through the App's settings.

## 2. Information We Collect
Because Aspend operates entirely locally on your device:
- **No Personal Information**: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers.
- **No Financial Data Collection**: We do not have access to your bank accounts, transaction history, or spending habits. Everything remains on your device.

## 3. Permissions
The App may request certain permissions to function effectively:
- **Notifications**: To alert you about budgets or important updates (managed entirely on your device).
- **Notification Access (Optional)**: If you enable "Auto-Detection," the App reads banking notifications locally to suggest transaction entries. This data is processed in real-time on your device and is never transmitted externally.
- **Storage/Files**: To allow you to export backups (CSV/JSON) or PDF reports to your device's storage.

## 4. Third-Party Services
Aspend does not share your data with any third-party services, advertisers, or analytics providers. 

## 5. Security
Your data's security is dependent on your device's security. We recommend using the App's "App Lock" feature (biometric/device authentication) to add an extra layer of protection to your financial data.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
